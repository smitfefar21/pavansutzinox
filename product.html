Chemical Information

  • CAS Number: 1314-13-2
  • Molecular Formula: ZnO
  • Molecular Weight: 81.38 g/mol
  • Appearance: White powder
  • Odor: Odorless

Typical Specifications

  • ZnO Content: 99.60% min
  • Lead (Pb): ≤ 2000 ppm
  • Cadmium (Cd): ≤ 60 ppm
  • Iron (Fe): ≤ 300 ppm
  • Loss on Ignition: ≤ 0.5%

Physical Properties

  • Density: 5.606 g/cm³
  • Melting Point: 1975°C
  • Solubility: Insoluble in water
  • Particle Size: 0.1-1.0 μm
  • Bulk Density: 0.5-1.0 g/cm³
