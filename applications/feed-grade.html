The Essential Role of Zinc in Animal Nutrition

Zinc is a critical micronutrient required for numerous biological functions in animals. Our feed grade Zinc Oxide provides a highly bioavailable source of this essential mineral.

Key Benefits in Animal Feed:

  • Growth Promotion: Essential for protein synthesis and cell division
  • Immune Function: Strengthens disease resistance
  • Reproductive Health: Improves fertility and conception rates
  • Hoof/Skin Integrity: Maintains epithelial tissue health
  • Antioxidant Protection: Part of superoxide dismutase enzyme
  • Gut Health: Reduces diarrhea in young animals

Swine Nutrition

Critical benefits for pig production:

  • Post-weaning: 2500-3000 ppm reduces diarrhea (first 2 weeks)
  • Growth: Improves average daily gain by 8-12%
  • Reproduction: Enhances sow fertility and litter size
  • Foot Health: Prevents lesions and lameness
  • Dosage: 50-150 ppm in complete feed

Poultry Nutrition

Essential for all poultry species:

  • Broilers: Improves FCR by 3-5%
  • Layers: Increases egg production and shell quality
  • Breeders: Enhances hatchability and chick viability
  • Immunity: Reduces mortality during disease challenges
  • Dosage: 60-120 ppm in complete feed

Ruminant Nutrition

Key benefits for cattle, sheep and goats:

  • Hoof Health: Prevents foot rot and laminitis
  • Fertility: Improves conception rates
  • Milk Quality: Reduces somatic cell counts
  • Stress: Helps manage heat stress effects
  • Dosage: 30-100 ppm in complete feed

Aquaculture Feeds

Important for fish and shrimp farming:

  • Growth: Increases weight gain by 10-15%
  • Survival: Reduces mortality during stress periods
  • Skin/Scale: Improves wound healing and scale integrity
  • Immunity: Enhances disease resistance
  • Dosage: 50-150 mg/kg in complete feed
