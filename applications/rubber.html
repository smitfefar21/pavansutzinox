Why Zinc Oxide is Essential in Rubber Manufacturing

Zinc Oxide plays a critical role as an activator in rubber vulcanization, significantly improving the physical properties of rubber products. Our Zinc Oxide is specially formulated for optimal performance in rubber applications.

Key Functions in Rubber Compounds:

  • Vulcanization Activator: Works with accelerators to optimize the cross-linking of rubber molecules
  • Heat Conductivity: Improves heat dissipation during processing and service
  • Mechanical Properties: Enhances tensile strength, elasticity, and abrasion resistance
  • Age Resistance: Protects against oxidative degradation and extends product life
  • Processing Aid: Improves mold flow and release characteristics

Enhancing Tyre Performance

Our Zinc Oxide significantly improves critical tyre characteristics:

  • Durability: Increases abrasion resistance for longer tread life
  • Heat Dissipation: Reduces heat buildup during operation
  • Wet Grip: Maintains optimal traction in wet conditions
  • Rolling Resistance: Helps achieve better fuel efficiency
  • Cure Rate: Optimizes vulcanization time in production

Recommended dosage: 3-5 phr (parts per hundred rubber)

Mechanical Rubber Goods

Essential for industrial rubber products requiring durability:

  • Seals & Gaskets: Improves compression set resistance
  • Anti-Vibration: Enhances damping properties
  • Conveyor Belts: Increases wear resistance
  • Molded Goods: Provides excellent mold flow

Recommended dosage: 2-5 phr depending on application

Footwear Applications

Improves performance in rubber footwear components:

  • Outsoles: Enhances abrasion resistance
  • Midsole: Maintains cushioning properties
  • Color Stability: Prevents yellowing in white/rubber
  • Processing: Reduces sticking to molds

Recommended dosage: 3-4 phr for optimal results

Hoses & Belts

Critical for high-performance rubber hoses and belts:

  • Flex Life: Increases bend resistance
  • Oil Resistance: Maintains properties in oil environments
  • Aging: Protects against ozone degradation
  • Adhesion: Improves fabric/rubber bonding

Recommended dosage: 4-6 phr for demanding applications
