Why Zinc Oxide is Preferred in Cosmetic Formulations

Zinc Oxide is a versatile, multifunctional ingredient that meets the growing demand for natural, effective, and safe cosmetic ingredients. Our high purity Zinc Oxide (up to 99.60%) is specially processed for cosmetic applications.

Key Benefits in Cosmetic Products:

  • Broad Spectrum UV Protection: Effective against both UVA and UVB radiation
  • Gentle on Skin: Non-irritating and suitable for sensitive skin
  • Natural Opacity: Provides coverage without heavy feel
  • Antimicrobial Properties: Helps preserve product integrity
  • Skin Benefits: Soothes irritation and supports healing
  • Regulatory Compliance: Meets global cosmetic standards

Sunscreen Formulations

Zinc Oxide is the gold standard for mineral sun protection:

  • Broad Spectrum: Protects against both UVA (320-400nm) and UVB (290-320nm)
  • Photostable: Doesn't degrade in sunlight like chemical filters
  • Reef-Safe: Environmentally friendly alternative to oxybenzone
  • Water Resistance: Enhances durability in water-resistant formulas
  • Formulation Flexibility: Works in creams, lotions, sticks, and sprays

Recommended dosage: 5-25% depending on SPF target

Makeup Products

Multifunctional benefits in color cosmetics:

  • Foundation: Provides natural opacity and SPF boost
  • BB/CC Creams: Combines coverage with sun protection
  • Powders: Improves slip and adhesion
  • Lip Products: Adds sun protection without taste
  • Mascara: Gentle alternative to carbon black

Recommended dosage: 2-15% depending on product type

Skincare Products

Therapeutic benefits beyond sun protection:

  • Acne Treatments: Soothes inflammation and reduces bacteria
  • Diaper Rash Creams: Forms protective barrier on skin
  • Anti-Aging: Prevents photoaging from UV exposure
  • After-Sun: Cools and calms sun-exposed skin
  • Barrier Creams: Protects against environmental stressors

Recommended dosage: 1-10% depending on function

Baby Care Products

Safe, gentle protection for delicate skin:

  • Baby Sunscreens: Recommended by pediatricians
  • Diaper Creams: Creates breathable protective layer
  • Baby Powders: Absorbs moisture without irritation
  • Baby Washes: Gentle cleansing with skin protection
  • Safety: Non-nano particles for minimized absorption

Recommended dosage: 5-15% in protective products
