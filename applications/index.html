Industry Spotlight: Rubber Manufacturing

Enhancing Rubber Products with Zinc Oxide

Zinc oxide plays a critical role in rubber vulcanization, improving:

  • Elasticity: Increases resilience and rebound
  • Durability: Enhances abrasion resistance
  • Heat Resistance: Improves thermal stability
  • Cure Rate: Optimizes vulcanization time

Our zinc oxide is specially formulated for optimal performance in rubber applications with 99% minimum purity.
