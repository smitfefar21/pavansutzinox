Why Zinc Oxide is Essential in Paint Formulations

Zinc Oxide serves as a multifunctional additive in paint and coating systems, providing both protective and aesthetic benefits. Our high purity Zinc Oxide is specially processed for optimal performance in coating applications.

Key Functions in Paints & Coatings:

  • UV Protection: Absorbs harmful UV radiation to prevent substrate degradation
  • Corrosion Inhibition: Provides cathodic protection for metal substrates
  • Mildew Resistance: Natural fungistatic properties inhibit mold growth
  • Opacity & Whiteness: Contributes to hiding power and brightness
  • Durability: Enhances chalk resistance and weatherability
  • Film Strength: Improves hardness and abrasion resistance

Architectural Coatings

Enhancing exterior and interior architectural paints:

  • Exterior Durability: 30% longer lifespan in harsh climates
  • Chalk Resistance: Maintains appearance over time
  • Mold Prevention: Reduces fungal growth on damp surfaces
  • Color Retention: Prevents fading from sun exposure
  • Washability: Improves scrub resistance

Recommended dosage: 2-10% by weight

Industrial Coatings

Protecting industrial equipment and structures:

  • Corrosion Control: ASTM B117 salt spray resistance
  • Chemical Resistance: Withstands harsh industrial environments
  • Abrasion Resistance: Protects against mechanical wear
  • Heat Stability: Maintains properties at elevated temperatures

Recommended dosage: 5-15% by weight

Marine Coatings

Specialized protection for marine environments:

  • Saltwater Resistance: Superior performance in marine atmospheres
  • Anti-Fouling: Reduces marine organism attachment
  • Humidity Tolerance: Prevents blistering in wet conditions
  • Intercoat Adhesion: Improves bonding between layers

Recommended dosage: 10-20% by weight

Automotive Coatings

Enhancing automotive finishes and undercoatings:

  • Stone Chip Resistance: Protects against road debris
  • UV Protection: Preserves clear coat integrity
  • Corrosion Protection: Zinc-rich primers for metal parts
  • Appearance: Maintains gloss and color depth

Recommended dosage: 3-8% by weight
